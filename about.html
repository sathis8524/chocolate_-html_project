ABOUT US

Waffle chocolates are a gourmet snack combining crisp, freshly baked waffle discs with rich coatings like india milk or dark chocolate. Often shaped like macarons, these treats offer a crunchy, indulgent experience, frequently featuring in-house, creamy chocolate sauces. They are popular as portable, ready-to-eat treats that blend traditional, warm waffle textures with decadent, velvety chocolate, often sold in various unique, innovative flavors.We all love our chocolates with a little bit of crunch! That’s exactly what we kept in mind when curating these perfectly crisp waffles coated in decadent, smooth, and creamy in-house chocolate sauces tasting absolutely delightful in every bite. A top-selling favorite amongst our customers, we did not anticipate this experiment receiving so much love. With multiple unique flavours to experiment with, these Waffle Chocolates are an absolute must-have.
